My Childhood

Athlete

I began my athletic career when I was two years old. I played baseball for my dad's little league team. I also began playing water polo when I was four-years-old. My brother began playing when he was a freshman in high school. Him playing made me want to play and I've been playing ever since. This year is my twelfth year playing water polo.
